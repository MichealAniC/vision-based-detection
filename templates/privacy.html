Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Information We Collect

Our system focuses on biometric identification. When you register, we collect:

  • Biometric Face Data: Captured through your camera to create a unique mathematical representation (histogram).
  • Identity Details: Your Full Name and unique Matric/Student ID.
  • Session Logs: Timestamps of when your attendance was recorded.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for educational attendance tracking:

  • Verifying your presence during official course sessions.
  • Generating attendance reports for lecturers and administrators.
  • Improving the accuracy of our recognition algorithm.

Data Security

We prioritize the safety of your biometric information:

  • Encryption: Face data is stored as encrypted histograms, not actual photographs.
  • Local Storage: All biometric processing and storage occur on secure local servers.
  • Access Control: Only authorized lecturers can view and export session logs.

Data Retention

We do not keep data longer than necessary. Attendance records are cleared at the end of each semester, and biometric data can be deleted upon graduation or withdrawal from the course.

User Consent

By enrolling your face in the system, you voluntarily consent to the collection and processing of your biometric data for the sole purpose of automated attendance tracking.

Contact

If you have questions about your data or wish to request data deletion, please contact the System Administrator at:

admin@facerecognition.system

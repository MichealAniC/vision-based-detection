Troubleshooting

Camera Not Detected

Check browser permissions and ensure no other app (like Zoom or Teams) is using the camera.

Poor Lighting

The AI needs clear features. Move to a brighter area or turn on room lights to improve recognition.

Multiple Faces

The system works best when only one face is in the frame. Ask others to step back during verification.

Slow Recognition

Ensure a stable internet connection. High latency can delay the biometric processing on the server.
